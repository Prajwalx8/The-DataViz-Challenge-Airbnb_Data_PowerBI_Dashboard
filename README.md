# 🏠 Airbnb Insights Dashboard – Chicago vs New Orleans

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes and compares Airbnb market trends between **Chicago** and **New Orleans**. The goal is to uncover meaningful insights related to **pricing, demand, host behavior, and neighbourhood performance** to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Perform comparative analysis between two cities  
- Understand pricing patterns and customer engagement  
- Identify top-performing hosts and neighbourhoods  
- Analyze supply vs demand dynamics  
- Deliver business insights through visualization  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔢 KPI Metrics
- Total Listings  
- Total Hosts  
- Total Reviews  
- Average Price  
- City-wise Listings (Chicago vs New Orleans)  

---

### 👤 Host Analysis
- Top 5 hosts by number of listings  
- Host-level performance comparison  

---

### 💰 Pricing & Demand Analysis
- Average price vs average reviews (city-wise)  
- **Supply vs Demand Trend** (availability vs reviews)  

---

### 🏘️ Property Analysis
- Room type distribution  
- Market share of entire homes, private rooms, etc.  

---

### 📍 Neighbourhood Analysis
- Top 10 neighbourhoods by listings  
- Top 3 neighbourhoods by price and reviews  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Chicago has a slightly higher number of listings, indicating a more competitive market  
- Entire homes dominate the platform, showing strong user preference for full-property stays  
- A small number of hosts manage a large share of listings, highlighting the presence of professional hosts  
- Listings with lower availability tend to receive more reviews, suggesting a **scarcity-driven demand effect**  
- Certain neighbourhoods act as premium zones with both high pricing and strong engagement  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

- Combined datasets from Chicago and New Orleans  
- Removed irrelevant columns (`neighbourhood_group`, `license`)  
- Handled missing values using mean, median, and mode imputation  
- Converted date columns into proper format  
- Ensured dataset consistency for visualization  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard design and visualization  
- **Google Colab / Jupyter Notebook** – Data analysis
